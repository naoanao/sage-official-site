## What are AI Agents?
AI agents are software programs that use machine learning algorithms to perform specific tasks, such as data analysis, customer service, or content creation. These agents can be trained to learn from data and improve their performance over time, making them an ideal solution for automating repetitive tasks and generating passive income. Some examples of AI agents include:

* Chatbots that provide customer support and answer frequently asked questions
* Virtual assistants that manage schedules and appointments
* Content generation tools that create blog posts, social media posts, and other types of content
* Investment algorithms that analyze market data and make trades
